My Hobbies and interests

My name is Gus and I'm 21 years old, I am currently studying to become a teacher at the Australian Catholic University (ACU) in Strathfield. He has chosen to specialise in teaching industrial technology, specifically timber, where I enjoy learning about woodworking, carpentry, and all things related to working with wood. I am passionate about passing on my knowledge and skills to future generations, hoping to inspire a love for hands-on craftsmanship in my future students.

Outside of my studies, I'm really passionate about rugby. Whether it's playing a match or watching rugby union or league, I can't get enough of the sport. I also love being outdoors and spending time in nature. There's nothing better than going camping, exploring new trails, or going off-roading with mates. Whether I'm tackling a challenging woodworking project or enjoying the great outdoors, I'm always up for an adventure.
